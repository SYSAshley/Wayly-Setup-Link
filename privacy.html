Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Wayly browser extension ("Wayly", "we", "us") handles information when you use our Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

What information we collect

The Wayly extension collects and stores only the following:

  • Configured URLs — the NetSuite account URLs you or your administrator enter during setup. These are stored locally in Chrome's sync storage.

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Browsing history
  • Personal identification information
  • NetSuite account credentials or data
  • Any content from pages you visit

How we use information

The configured URLs are used solely to determine which pages the Wayly learning widget should appear on. This information never leaves your browser except to sync across your own Chrome devices via Chrome's built-in sync feature.

Third-party services

Wayly loads a learning widget from Stonly (stonly.com) on configured NetSuite pages. By using Wayly, the Stonly widget script is loaded on those pages. Please refer to Stonly's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data.

Content Security Policy modification

To allow the Stonly widget to load on NetSuite pages, the extension removes Content-Security-Policy headers on your configured URLs. This is required for technical compatibility and only applies to the specific URLs you configure.

Data storage

Your configured URLs are stored using Chrome's storage.sync API, which means they sync across your Chrome browsers if you are signed into Chrome. This data is managed entirely by Google's Chrome sync infrastructure.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. No data is transmitted to Wayly's servers.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
